CollegeDiary

  • Tech Stack: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap,JS, Python, Flask , Mongodb, AWS S3

The Integrated Annual Academic Calendar will be a web-based system accessible to all Indian universities. It should integrate with existing university systems, if applicable, to gather relevant data. The calendar system will operate independently and provide a user-friendly interface for universities, staff, and students to access and manage their academic schedules.

- Centralized academic calendar accessible to all Indian universities - Management of academic schedules, holidays, examination periods, and events - Integration with existing university systems, if applicable - User roles and permissions for administrators, academic staff, and students - Notifications and reminders for important academic events
